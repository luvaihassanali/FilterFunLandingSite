Support

Read opening message carefully to understand application's limitations. The removal/distortion of audio in restuling videos is hardware based. I tested this app using iPhone XS and know that Static Effect and Comic Effect filters removes audio completely. Testing on older phones such as the SE reveal that most filters cannot record with audio. The camera can still function regularly and while outputting audio from other apps however. Photo and video quality is set to highest available preset on the phone but video writing is specifically set to 720x1280 resolution and photo quality outputs lower than usual due to internal methods required for applying filters. The application uses resources heavily and will trigger warnings when phone system pressure is elevated (heat is indicator). Heed the warnings; I am not responsible for any damages. More information can be found here at Apple's developer documentation.
