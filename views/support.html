Support

Read the opening message carefully to understand application's limitations. The removal/distortion of audio in videos is hardware based. Tests on all iPhone versions reveal that Static Effect and Comic Effect filters removes audio completely. Testing on older phones such as the SE reveal that the app cannot record audio using the Main Camera, but can while on the Selfie Camera. Additionally, there is some lag experienced on older phones while changing filter or switching orientation while recording.

Refer to the tables below for sound output testing details. All models cannot take still pictures while on Static Filter as the result is just a white image. Video writing is done in 720p HD (1280x720) resolution and photo quality outputs lower than usual due to internal methods required for applying filters. The application uses resources heavily and will trigger warnings when phone system pressure is elevated (heat is indicator). Heed the warnings; I am not responsible for any damages. More information can be found here at Apple's developer documentation.

For all versions:
    • Results will vary depending on phone status (number of apps open, running time, etc.)
    • No audio when recording with either Comic Effect or Static Effect filters
    • Cannot take still pictures while on Static Effect
    • Kaleidoscope and Bump Distortion filter may not be centered on iPhones (8 and lower) with screens smaller or iPads
